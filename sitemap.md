# Page 3: Services
Section 1: Project Management & Redevelopment At Cubic, globally certified project managers handle your projects professionally. Our redevelopment services (self or through a builder) include:
• Defining the scope of various agencies.
• Finalizing milestones and baseline schedules.
• Preparing comparative statements and finalizing vendors.
• Monitoring and controlling project execution while administering contracts.
Section 2: Turnkey Construction & Contracting Our expertise lies in concrete, steel structures, sterile areas, and vacuum de-watered floors. We provide engineering, procurement, and construction for:
• Residential: High Rise Towers, Colonies, Bungalows.
• Industrial: Pharmaceutical, Chemical, Textile, and Manufacturing plants.
• Commercial & Infrastructure: IT parks, healthcare facilities, roads, and drainage.
Section 3: Structural Audits & Repairs Consulting We conduct highly detailed, BMC-required structural audits. Our services include:
• Project investigation and non-destructive testing to indicate areas of repair.
• Recommending quantified structural repairs and rehabilitation.
• Assisting societies in selecting the right contractors and supervising works for quality assurance.
Section 4: Architecture & Valuation Our internationally qualified architects design functional and impressive spaces. Furthermore, our government-certified valuers provide accurate property valuations to enable your financial goals.
Section 5: Corporate Training, Consulting & Software We accelerate organizational performance through project management and civil engineering training. Additionally, we develop customized B2B and B2C software solutions tailored to the civil engineering sector.
